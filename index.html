Service Programs

Choose a program model that matches your operations.

Routine Program

Daily, weekly, or custom-frequency cleaning for properties that require consistent upkeep and presentation.

  • Site onboarding and baseline walkthrough
  • Fixed checklist with supervisor sign-off
  • Monthly service review with your contact

Intensive Program

Periodic deep cleaning for high-traffic areas, seasonal refreshes, and priority hygiene zones.

  • Detailed scope for floors, washrooms, and touchpoints
  • Low-disruption scheduling outside peak hours
  • Pre- and post-service quality checklist

Handover Program

Comprehensive end-of-lease and handover cleans for offices, retail tenancies, and commercial units.

  • Vacate-ready detailing across key surfaces and fittings
  • Targeted tasks aligned to handover expectations
  • Final review against agreed scope of work
